About valUProp.in

valUProp.in was built to answer one simple question every Indian property buyer, seller, and investor asks: "What is my property really worth?"

Professional appraisals cost ₹5,000–₹25,000 and take days. Bank valuations are conservative and process-heavy. We built valUProp to give you a credible, methodology-backed estimate in 60 seconds, at ₹99.

Our approach is land-led — the same methodology used by registered valuers in India. We combine publicly available pricing signals, government guideline values, RERA data, and our proprietary locality price database with AI to generate each report.

valUProp is not a replacement for a registered valuer. We are a decision-support tool for the 99% of Indians who need a fast, honest, and affordable starting point.
