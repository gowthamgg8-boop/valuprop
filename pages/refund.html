Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2025

7-day no-questions-asked refund

We offer a full refund of ₹99 within 7 days of purchase, no questions asked. We believe in our product — if you are not satisfied, we want to make it right.

How to request a refund

Email support@valuprop.in with subject line "Refund Request" and include your payment ID (from your Razorpay receipt). We will process the refund within 5–7 business days via the original payment method.

After 7 days

Refunds are not available after 7 days from the date of purchase, except in cases of technical failure on our part (report not delivered, payment processed but report not generated). In such cases, contact us immediately at support@valuprop.in.
