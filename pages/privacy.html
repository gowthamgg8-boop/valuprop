Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

valUProp.in ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information in compliance with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 (DPDP Act).

Data we collect

When you use valUProp.in, we collect: your phone number and email address (at payment step), property details you enter (location, area, type, age), payment confirmation data from Razorpay, and device/browser information for analytics.

How we use your data

Your data is used solely to: generate your property valuation report, email you the PDF report, send follow-up communications you consent to, and improve our valuation model.

We never sell your data

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We share data only with Razorpay (payment processing) and AWS SES (email delivery).

Your rights

Under the DPDP Act 2023, you have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal data. Email info@myriky.com to exercise these rights.
